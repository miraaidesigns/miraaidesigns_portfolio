About Me

I'm Sriya Jami, a passionate and detail-oriented interior design graduate with a strong foundation in space planning, color theory, CAD, and 3D visualization tools. I hold a Diploma in Interior Design from Hamstech and a BBA from GITAM School of Business. I am currently working as a Designer at Studio 9 yards, where I contribute to creative and client-focused residential and commercial projects.



During my internship at Lotus Interior Designs, I worked on 2D & 3D design drawings, material selection, and client consultations. My strengths include effective communication, sketching, and using tools like SketchUp, Enscape, photoshop, Excel, and Canva. With a creative mindset and a hands-on approach, I aim to transform ideas into functional, aesthetic living spaces and continuously grow with every project I take on.
